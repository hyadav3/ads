About

Diabetes is a prevalent and complex health condition, and understanding the relationships between various medical parameters and diabetes progression is critical for effective patient management and healthcare decision-making. The Python Diabetes dataset contains relevant data, but its potential insights can be challenging to unlock without proper tools. The Web-Based Diabetes Dataset Visualization Application is an innovative project aimed at developing a user-friendly web application to visualize and analyze the Python Diabetes dataset. This project responds to the growing need for accessible tools to explore healthcare data and extract valuable insights related to diabetes and its contributing factors

Data and Methods

Data

The data used in this project was collected from the National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases. It was designed to collect information to predict whether a patient has diabetes. All patients are female and at least 21 years old. The data is available from download from Kaggle.

This website was created as a final project for Applied data science for the Fall 2023 semester.
